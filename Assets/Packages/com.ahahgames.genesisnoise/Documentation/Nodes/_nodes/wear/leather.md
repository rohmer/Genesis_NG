Leather has a very distinct wear signature compared to fabric or stone:
- 	Crease brightening
- 	Oil darkening in cavities
- 	Scuffing along direction of motion
- 	Crackle micro-cracks

- 	Pore darkening
- 	Dryness (desaturation + roughness increase)
- 	Micro-grain breakup
